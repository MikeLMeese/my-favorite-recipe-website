Stir-fried Eggs with Tomatoes

Stir-fried eggs with Tomatoes is one of my personal favorite dishes of all time. The slight acidic tang of the tomatoes coupled with the richness of the egg and the smell of green onion is always a treat for the mouth! Not only that, it is one of the simplest dishes to make, in as little as 15 minutes!

As I stated earlier, this recipe is quick and simple, perfect for a weeknight meal. Not to mention that is extremely nutritious and delicious!
Gather all of the necessary ingredients first. Then follow the directions here to whip up a delicious serving for yourself and others!
