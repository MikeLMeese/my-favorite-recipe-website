Ingredients for Stir-fried Eggs with Tomatoes

  • 4 large eggs
  • 5 medium tomatoes
  • 2-3 green onion
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Sugar (to counteract the tangy sourness of the tomatoes)
  • 2 tbsp Cooking oil
